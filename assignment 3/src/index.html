FACTS YOU MAY NOT KNOW ABOUT BLACK BEARS

  1. the scientific name is Ursus Americanus
  2. the American Black Bear is related to Brown, Polar, Sun, and Sloth Bear
  3. this bear is under high risk of extinction
  4. in North America there are almost 600000 black bears
  • their height goes from 4 to 7 feet
  • their weight goes from 200 to 600 pounds
  • breeding normally is between may and august
  • birth goes from January to starting of February
  • baby pairs are very common and their weight is around 1 pound

DESCRIPTION

The American Bear in adult age, his skull is almost 30 cm, the claws are black, gray, or brown, the claws are short and round. The paws, on the other side are larger comparing then with the other bears paws apart from the polar and brown bears. The ears are small and rounded in the backside of the head. Their weight depends mainly on their age, health, sex, and season. For example male's weight is much higher than female bears, in autumn their weight is higher than in spring, adult bears are heavier than young bears. BEHAVIOR

The American black bears's eyesight and hearing is much better what humans have, the smell sense is that strong than is 7 times more sensitive than a our dog's sense. These bears can swim very well, they normally swim for food, for example hunting for fishes, or for pleasure. On the other side this animal can even climb trees, the main reason for climbing trees is normally to hide from enemies, for food, or for hibernate. They tend to be territorial, their territories are marked by rubbing and clawing against the trees. These bears communicate with each other by some vocal sounds and some non-vocal sounds, different type of sounds are made in cordial situations, on the other side when they moan or blow air they are nervous or angry.

ATTACKS

The american bears throughout the years have always been able to kill humans, but they prefer maintaining any type of fight with humans This bears are dangerous but not as dangerous as grizzly bears. The black bears does not attack humans normally, they mock. American black bears normally don't attack for territory, they normally hunt only for food, so for their survival, the worst attack recorded is in May 1978, when an American Black Bear attacked 3 youngsters that were fishing in Algonquin Park in Canada and got killed on the moment. An other attack recorded in August 1997 when in Liard River Hot Springs Provincial Park in Canada when a black bear attacked and killed a child a mother and a man who tried to help, and a the bear was killed while he was mauling a fourth person. The American black bear as many other animals have been hunted, the first Americans that lived in America started hunting those animals, for many NATI tribes these bears were considered as very intelligent and when they killed those bears they decorated their heads because they considered them as very intelligent creature.

DIFFERENCES BETWEEN RUSSIAN AND AMERICAN BEARS

Location

in Russia bears mostly live in forest zones of Siberia, specially in the north zone where it's colder, in the south side of Russia there aren't many bears because the weather and the fauna aren't suitable for them, on the other side the American Bear lives mostly in the Alaska zone.

Appearance

in USA their weight can reach 13 feet and they are one of the most aggressive and dangerous animals in the world, the main difference is that they have long hair, on the other side in Russia the bears have short brown hair, they are much smaller and weaker than the north American bears.

Image

The american bears have a very bad image, they attack people, and in the movie The Revenant, they attacked Leonardo DiCaprio, the russian bears are have a very decent image, and they were even the symbol of the 1980 Summer Olympics in Moscow.

Claws

American bear's claws can be 6 inches long, the russian ones are much shorter, and they don't hunt with their claws but with their teeth.

Work out

russian bears can easily climb trees, because their weight is much lower than the american bears and their claws are smaller.

Diet

both bears are omnivorous but they prefer eating plants, nuts, and fresh salmon. The differences are only the type of plants and fishes available in those certain places.

Tail

russian bear's tail on average a bit longer than the american bear.
